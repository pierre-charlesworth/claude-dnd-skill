THE PALE LEDGER
A Coastal Campaign
Session 14
The Drowned Cup · 21:47
Scene — Dockside Standoff

The Drowned Cup goes quiet by degrees — first the dice, then the laughter, then the fiddler who lets his bow drift off the string. Ben sets his cup down without a sound. Across the warped oak table, Sorn has not touched hers.

Her hand rests flat beside it, fingers spread, deliberate. The lamplight catches a thin chain at her wrist and the three pale tokens strung along it — bone, or something close to it.

Sorn I have pieces too. Some of yours match mine. So — what exactly did he show you?

Somewhere behind the bar a stool scrapes. Two dockhands have stopped pretending to drink. Ben's thumb finds the rapier's guard under the table — the old reflex.
